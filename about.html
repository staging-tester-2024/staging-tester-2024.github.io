Hi, I am a Swift and JavaScript developer. I started coding in September 2015, when I began my journey as a JS programmer. Later on I also started learning Swift and creating iOS apps. My first app, Swimrun World, was accepted on the AppStore in December 2018 and this experience has encouraged me to keep coding.

In April 2019 I released Runny, a running app, to help with the effectivity of your run, followed by Relays, the world's first chat app written in SwiftUI. In November 2020, my app Eveny got featured on the AppStore in "Widgets get an upgrade" story, fulfilling my dream. Aside from coding, I am an avid runner, climber and from time to time I fire up After Effects and try some motion design.
